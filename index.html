About Me

I'm an animation student based in New York City, and I have experience in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Animate, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Audition, as well as Autodesk Maya.

I have a penchant for the edgy and wicked type of artwork, as well as the more laid-back yet lively sort. I also have a soft spot for graffiti and murals, because I believe street art is the soul of the city. I aim to make life a hint less boring and a hint more cool with my work. And I'd like for you to see me do it, too.
