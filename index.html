About Me

My name is Travis Camacho, and I'm an animation student based in New York City.

I have a penchant for the edgy and wicked type of artwork, as well as the more laid-back yet lively sort. I also have a soft spot for graffiti and murals, because to me, street art is the soul of the city.

I aim to make life a hint less boring and a hint more cool with my work. And I'd like for you to see me do it, too.
